FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions about our performance model.

RaaS (Revenue as a Service) works in 3 steps: 1) We build your professional website (entry fee 299-399/year). 2) We bring you new clients through SEO, GEO, and AI. 3) You pay only 3% on the revenue generated by the new contacts we bring. Your personal dashboard lets you verify everything in real-time.
The 3% is calculated on the total revenue generated by new leads/contacts that we bring to your business through our SEO, GEO, and AI strategies. Every lead is tracked via UTM parameters, dedicated forms, and integrated CRM. Quarterly reconciliation with audit rights for both parties.
Every client gets access to a personal dashboard where they can verify in real-time: website visitors, generated leads, acquired contacts, SEO performance, and attributed revenue. Everything is transparent, everything is verifiable. The contract includes quarterly reconciliation and audit rights.
We don't use WordPress or heavy CMS platforms. We develop in optimized pure code (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) that guarantees PageSpeed 90+ on Google. Faster websites = better SEO ranking, more conversions, and happier users.
GEO is the next evolution of SEO. While traditional SEO optimizes for Google search results, GEO optimizes your content for AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. In 2026, 48% of Google queries show AI-generated answers. We ensure your business is visible and cited by AI when potential clients ask questions about your industry.

2. Privacy Notice (GDPR)

2.1 Types of Data Collected

We collect the following data when you use our website:

  • Contact data: first name, last name, email, phone number, company name, VAT number (provided via contact forms, newsletter, or grant aggregator subscriptions)
  • Browsing data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, visit duration
  • Payment data: processed directly by PayPal and Stripe (we do not store credit card data)
  • Technical and analytical cookies: to improve user experience and analyse traffic

2.2 Purposes of Processing

We use your data to:

  • Respond to information requests or quote enquiries
  • Provide you with the requested services (websites, grant aggregator, automations)
  • Manage subscriptions and payments for the Grant Aggregator service
  • Send you marketing communications and newsletters (only with explicit consent)
  • Improve the website and services through statistical analysis
  • Comply with legal and accounting obligations

2.3 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Explicit consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR): for marketing and newsletters
  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR): for requested services and subscriptions
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR): for improving our services
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6.1.c GDPR): for tax and accounting obligations

2.4 Data Retention

  • Contact data and newsletter: up to 24 months from the last interaction or withdrawal of consent
  • Contractual and subscription data: 10 years for tax obligations (as required by Italian law)
  • Marketing data: until withdrawal of consent
  • Logs and browsing data: maximum 12 months

2.5 Data Recipients

  • Supabase Inc. (cloud database, EU servers)
  • PayPal Holdings Inc. and Stripe Inc. (payment processing)
  • Formspree (contact form management)
  • Google LLC (Analytics, with anonymised IP)
  • Legal, tax, and accounting advisors
  • Public authorities when required by law

2.6 Your Rights (Art. 15-22 GDPR)

  • Access: obtain confirmation and a copy of your data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: restrict the processing of your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal: withdraw your consent at any time

How to exercise your rights: Send an email to info@raasautomazioni.it with the subject line "GDPR Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

2.7 Complaints

You may file a complaint with the Italian Data Protection Authority (Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali), the supervisory authority under GDPR for data processing carried out by Italian-based controllers:
Piazza Venezia 11, 00187 Roma, Italy - Email: garante@gpdp.it - Web: www.garanteprivacy.it

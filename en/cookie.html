1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to improve the browsing experience and to remember your preferences.

2. Cookies Used

3. Third-Party Cookies

The website may load external resources that set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts: for loading typefaces. Google may collect anonymous technical data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: for authentication and data management. Supabase Privacy Policy

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Disabling technical cookies may affect the proper functioning of the website.
